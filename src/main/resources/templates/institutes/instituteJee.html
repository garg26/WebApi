Institute Highlights

Motion is one of the premier coaching institutes of India, providing classroom and distance learning coaching for JEE (Main), JEE (Advanced), Pre- medical (NEET- UG/ AllMS) and Early Lead foundation courses (Class V to X, NTSE & Olympiads). Motion started its historic journey in the year 2007 and since its inception, it has aimed to give exceptional coaching to its students and produce top results in all Engineering and Medical entrance exams.
