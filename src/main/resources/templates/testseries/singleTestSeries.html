Essay Test Series for UPSC Mains 2018
  1. Essay paper plays an important role in the UPSC Mains examination and it is also one of determining factor in Mains exam.
  2. Essay Test Series includes 8 Essay tests on different topics relevant for USPC Mains exam preparation.
  3. Essay Test copies are evaluated by S. Ansari and Prof. Mujtaba Hussain.
  4. They provide their valuable feedback for improvement in Essay Writing.

The course will cover various types of Essay topics such as related to Polity & governance, Indian Society, Economics, Science and Tech etc. It will help in developing the structure and flow in Essay writing and will allow practice different dimensions of Essay Topic.
